Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or any data beyond what you explicitly enter.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling filters. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access. You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all associated data from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, but these providers are contractually prohibited from accessing or using your data for any other purpose.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channels. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly.
